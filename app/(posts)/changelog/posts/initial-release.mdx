# Initial Release

## Features

- Add and manage RSS feeds in Discord channels
- Receive AI-powered summaries of new posts
- Customize feed update frequency
- Simple slash commands for all operations

## Known Issues

- Limited to 10 feeds per channel currently
- Occasionally slow response times during peak hours
